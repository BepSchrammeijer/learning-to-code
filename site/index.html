Our menu

soya

A protein rich legume, now grown at large scale. Despite being good food for humans tends to disappear into the stomachs of many more cows and pigs. Often eaten in the form of tofu, or more recently as fake meat.

beans

An age old protein source. The many different varieties of beans can be cooked up into many different meals.

funghi

Funghi, or the many varieties of mushrooms, can be found in the wild, however it is safer to purchase them from people who know which ones are edible, or stick to the commonly known types.
